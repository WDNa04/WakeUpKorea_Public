# Privacy Policy for WakeUpKorea

Last updated: July 2026

## Overview
WakeUpKorea is a proximity-based transit alarm app designed for use in South Korea. **We are committed to protecting your privacy.**

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
WakeUpKorea collects your device's GPS location while the app is in use and in the background. This data is used solely to calculate your distance to your chosen destination and trigger an alarm when you are approaching it.

- Location data is processed entirely on your device
- Location data is never sent to our servers
- Location data is never stored, sold, or shared with third parties

### Search Queries
When you search for a destination, your search query is sent to our secure backend server, which forwards it to Naver's Local Search API to retrieve location results. Search queries are not stored or logged.

## Information We DO NOT COLLECT
- We do not collect your name, email, or any personal information.
- We do not require account creation.
- We do not use advertising.
- We do not track your location history.

## Data Storage
- Saved favorite locations are stored locally on your device only.
- Search history is stored locally on your device only.
- No user data is stored on our servers.

## Third Party Services
WakeUpKorea uses the following third party service:
- **Naver Local Search API** — for location search. Subject to [Naver's Privacy Policy](https://www.naver.com/rules/privacy.html)
- **Naver Maps API** - for accurate distance calculation. Subject to [Naver's Privacy Policy](https://www.naver.com/rules/privacy.html)

## Children's Privacy
While WakeUpKorea is suitable for usage for all ages, we especially do not knowingly collect data from children. 

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
